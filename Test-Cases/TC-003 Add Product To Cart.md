# TC-003 Add Product To Cart

## Module

Products

## Objective

Verify that a product can be added to the shopping cart.

## Preconditions

- User is logged in.

## Test Data

Product: Sauce Labs Backpack

## Test Steps

1. Log in to SauceDemo.
2. Locate the product.
3. Click "Add to Cart".
4. Open the shopping cart.

## Expected Result

The selected product appears in the shopping cart.

## Status

Pass
