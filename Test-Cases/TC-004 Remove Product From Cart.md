# TC-004 Remove Product From Cart

## Module

Shopping Cart

## Objective

Verify that a product can be removed from the shopping cart.

## Preconditions

- User is logged in.
- At least one product is in the cart.

## Test Data

Product: Sauce Labs Backpack

## Test Steps

1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click "Remove" on the selected product.

## Expected Result

The product is removed from the shopping cart.

## Status

Pass
